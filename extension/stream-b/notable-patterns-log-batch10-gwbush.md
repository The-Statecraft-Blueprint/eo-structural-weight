### Chunk 2 (EO 13223-13248, 25 EOs) — complete: the immediate post-9/11 institutional response, including the landmark terrorism-financing order and a controversial reversal of presidential-records transparency

**The foundational post-9/11 national-security cluster.** EO13223 (Ready Reserve activation, 30.0%) is the operational launch order for the military reserve mobilization, issued the same day as the President's national-emergency proclamation, delegating mobilization authority to the Secretaries of Defense and Transportation "without... action by the President" (Flag1).

# Findings

Qualitative, cross-order discoveries — recurring drafting templates, structural patterns, named phenomena — surfaced by actually reading the text at scale, rather than the validation claim itself. This folder is expected to grow substantially: full-justification coding across 91 years and roughly 7,150 orders (see `../extension/`) surfaces a lot more than the 298-order validation sample did on its own, and much of what's in the per-batch notable-patterns logs under `../extension/stream-a/` and `../extension/stream-b/` hasn't yet been synthesized into a proper cross-referenced write-up here.

## What belongs here versus elsewhere

This folder is for **patterns**, not for the validation claim or the extension's raw data. If a finding is about *how well structural weight tracks Mayer & Price's classification*, it belongs in `../mayer-price-validation/comparison-to-mayer-price.md`. If it's a specific order's coding record, it lives in the relevant batch file. This folder is where a pattern gets named, defined, and tracked across every instance found — the kind of thing that's genuinely new knowledge about how executive orders get drafted, independent of any one order's significance.
